The Process

Overview

Behind The Pen is an online art platform that presents artwork from a variety of perspectives, including completed pieces, artist biographies, creative processes, and community involvement.

Brainstorming

This shows the beginning phase of Behind The Pen, where concepts, themes, colors, and key elements were collected and organized.

Sitemap

The sitemap shows the main pages and subpages so visitors can move through the site in a clear, user-friendly way.

Wireframe

The wireframe lays out the navigation, images, text, and content sections before adding colors and final graphics.

Moodboard

The moodboard helped compare visual directions and choose a look that fit the website's tone.

Iterations

Sketches and alternate layouts made it easier to test ideas before moving into final mockups.

3 Different Approaches

Different design directions helped compare color, layout, and mood before choosing the strongest final direction.
